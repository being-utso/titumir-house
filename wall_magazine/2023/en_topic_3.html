'Life', A Never Ending Dream

~ Cadet Abdullah, 2223



Life, maybe a magical moment of quitting dreams with threads of misery, struggle, experience, sacrifice and many many colors we want to add, or, maybe our hearts struggling to pump below the ribcage.


In life, sometimes, a ray of hope enlightens the heart and sometimes pessimistic realization darkens it. In our childhood, we have huge lands of dreams in our heart. But with the passage of time, reality tears the ocean lands into pieces. Still some people continue dreaming and crawling for something big. Such a dreamer is like a lantern who dreams to shine like the stars high up in the sky, but also fears falling from that height someday. Nothing is certain in this world. But that small percentage of ascension chance is enough encouragement to make efforts.


Achieving success is leaving a mark on the path but life is the journey. In life, success is like dim candle that is very hard to find. After achieving it, one can use it to light up a civilization, a society or at least another candle- the success of another person. We are not here to enjoy our own success, rather it’s our responsibility to help and guide our next generations towards new success.


The world is like a mountain. People climb it following different routes. All are given the same tools. Some become sated at lower heights and stays there until their heart stops. Some take risky decisions get clumsy on their feet and ultimately fall down. Leaving a flag at the summit of that mountain is what fulfilling the dreams means. Reaching there is tough, only the strong willed can fulfill their dreams. This toil some journey towards the summit is life, the ‘alp’ of risks, a jeopardy, to survive hope and optimism are the only drops of water in the long walk- a walk to reach the summit that a very few dare to take.


Dreams are like the thousand new mirages in the Sahara desert where we still find joy running towards it. Sometimes we end up in sandstorm. But every time, the mirages take us a step closer to the Atlantic of dreams. Maybe some days we can reach the Atlantic of our dreams. Maybe, that will become a twinkling star in the night sky.
