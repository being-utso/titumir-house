Fidel Castro

~ Cadet Azmaeen, 2322



Fidel Alejandro Castro Ruz was a Cuban revolutionary and politician born in Biran , Cuba on August 13, 1926.He was the son of wealthy Spanish farmer Castro y Argiz . He started his education background from Roman Catholic Boarding School. He was trained as a military lawyer and received a doctorate of law from, the Havana University. Castro was a communist leader. He was the prime minister of Cuba from 1959 to 1976 and president from 1976 to 2008. He made Cuba the first socialist country of the western Hemisphere. He served as a secretary of communist party of Cuba from 1965 to 2011. During the cold war he was an ally of the Soviet Union. After participating in rebellions against right wing governments in the Dominican Republic and Colombia, he planned the overthrow of Cubian President Fulgencia Batista launching a failed attack on the Moncada Barracks in 1953. The USA came to propose Castro’s govt. and unsuccessfully attempted to over throw him by assassination, economic embargo, and country revolution, including the Bay of Pigs invasion of 1961.Counting all the threats, Castro allligned with Soviet Union and allowed the Soviets to place nuclear weapon in Cuba, resulting in the “Cuban missile crisis”-a defining incidents of cold war in 1962. The crisis was eventually neutralized by means of peaceful talks. Castro supported anti-imperialism hacking the establishment of Marxist governments in in chile. Nicaragua and Cerenada . As the longest serving non royale head of state in the 20th and 21st centuries castro polarized world. His supporters view him as a champion of socialist whose revolutionary government advanced economic and social justice who sescuring Cuba’s independence from American hegemony. Some people call Castro a dictator. They criticize him as he was accused by the westerners of violation of human rights. But his supporters were the most. Nelson Mendela has spoken in favour of him and has thanked him for Aid to Africa. After completing all the works for Cuba, he handed over provision power in July 2006 because of health.This great leader who shows Cuba the path of revolutionary left the world on November 26 , 2016 an age of 90 in Havana , Cuba . Our father of the nation Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman met Fidel Castro in the UN assembly. There they shared their philosophy, struggle and prospects. Castro complimented on Bangabandhu , “ I have not seen the Himalayas , But I have seen Bangabandhu. In terms of character and personality, He was the Himalayas.”
