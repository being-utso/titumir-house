Memories of the Martyrs

~ Cadet Faysal, 2175



We mourn our language martyrs-
Our brothers who gave us a start,
The few who stood against tyranny
And faced the oppressor’s dart.
They defended our right to be free,
To speak in our mother tongue;
Though they were lost on that fateful day,
Our legacy has now been won.
Today, we honour their courage-
For the fight and for the pain.
Let us remember that fateful day
And never forget again.
Let us raise our voices in tribute
To those who thought us strong,
And let us vow to keep their memories alive
As we move ever on!
