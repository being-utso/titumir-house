Matarbari Deep Sea Port

~ Cadet Tamim, 2377



With the gradual development of the world, import-export and diplomatic relation among the countries is increasing and becoming more intimate. Not only that the modern world requires more electricity production and advancement of technology to advance further to uphold the strength of our motherland ,the government has taken several mega projects to make the international and infranational communication and transportation esier among these mega projects the first deep sea port of our country,Matarbari deep sea port is one of the most citable and costly projects in the whole country whose bufget is Tk.17777.20 crore which is nearly 3times of the Padma bridge .This deep sea port is located at moheshkhali ,Cox’s bazar .The main objective of this mega project is to strengthen the port logistics capacity of Bangladesh and ensure supply of sufficient goods in a short time. A deep sea port is comperatively profitable than normal seaports for its transportation facility as the channel depth is very much danger in the dep sea. Previously with 10-12m of deep channels the goods needed to be released from the mother vessel in Srilanka or Singapore ports and then small vessel used to carry them in Chittagong Sea port which was very much costly. But now there is no need for good aquittai in the foreign country as the depth of the channel of the deep sea port is 16-18m .Now mother vessel can directly carry 8000-10000 TEV(Twenty foot equivalent).At a time normal vessel could carry only 800-2400 TEU goods. This sea port is being built with resemblance of Japanese sea ports of Kashima and Niigata.There will be 300m or 500ft long multipurposed terminal and one 460m or 1510ft long container built by 2026.The navigation channel will be 350m or 1150ft length with a minimum permissible draught of only 18m.This project will highly be facilitated SMD on sediment mitigation Dyke which will not let the sediments to port area and the mother vessels will be able to dock themselves in this deep port channel . already as a significant footmark in the history of Bangladesh the largest trading vessel docked in this coastal country is owusu maru in this very deep sea port on 25th April,2023. On the other hand ,wave controlling dyke and X shaped blocks with boulder base is specially built for preventing unsual wave and sediment .The matabari deep sea port was inaguarated on 11th November , 2023.by the prime minister of Bangladesh , Shekh Hasina . ECNEC approved this project on the 10th march 2020 and construction started on 16 November 2020 and with suggestion of Japanese company Nippon Sieno Company limited this project is being built in the coast of Bay of Bengal .In September 2020, japan International cooperation Agency won the contract for the consultancy services of this project. Two contracts were signed by Roads and Highways department (RHD) with oriental consultants Global Company Limited and by Chittagong port authority with Nippon Koei In this sea port there will be a 14703 Km and 350m long navigation channel for the purpose of carrying the goods . there will be terminal built on 17 Hectors with a 300m berth and able to accommodate ships upto 7000 DWT ( Deadweight tonnage ) . One of the main purpose to build this port in Matarbari is to supply sufficient coal to the Matarbari Ultra Super project or in short Matarbari Powerplant. Coal is needed to be imported because 12000 tons is needed per day in this large powerplant where Barapukuria Powerstation can only supply 4000 ton per day .In this 1200 MW powerplant there manufactures 1800 ton steam per day .Again the transportation coast of non processed coal without transportation 16000 tk metric ton but Indonesia is giving as processed 2 inch on 50mm coal in only 200% on 22000tk pen metric ton . this economic facility her made it as a multipurposed port which can keep a vital role of 2-3% in GDP. Moreover this port is going to change the whole economy of Bangladesh by creating more employment sectors and making import and export smooth . The communication is also easy from this port . In the past vessel needed 45 days to reach USA , the top buyer garments. But from this port this will b e only a 23 days journey . By this port Bangladesh is entering into the renaissance period as a self dependent country . From 'bottomless basket' of Kissinger , its becoming Bangabandhu’s Sonar Bangla . This gradual development will make a Friday for our future .
