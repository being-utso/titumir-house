Anecdotes from Jerusalem

~ Cadet Mahmud, 2259



The azure sky is filled with smoke,
Kalashnikovs are still roaring.
The hungry bullets rush ruthlessly
Towards the orphans weeping.

They say, “Mission Accomplished”
With menacing triumph of the devil.
That symphony raptures the earth beneath-
Shrilling the quake of evil.

Corpses and ruins around-
Chorus the chronicles of atrocities,
Wails echo from dusk to dawn,
Fire glazes on the shackles that besiege.

The shackles never get rusty
Enslaving the misery sky.
Rebel bullets rage, but,
Budding dreams can’t fly.

The dreams wanna break free
Shattering the ribcage.
Fists rise against the sinister-
Pretending to be sage.

But the saviors of justice
Handicap those Fists.
Hypocrites Hercules’ of morality
Tag them as terrorists.

Justice ha groveled today-
In front of jury clowns.
Rebels are searching for a new horizon;
For revenge at every ounce.
